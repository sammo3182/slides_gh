Interview Methods

Why being interpretivist

要克服自弹自唱、自娱自乐、隔空喊话、封闭运行的倾向，进万家门、访万家情、结万家亲，经常同群众进行面对面、手拉手、心贴心的零距离接触，增进对群众的真挚感情。要克服以主观想象代替群众真实需求的倾向，把握群众所需所急所盼，少搞一些强加于群众的活动，多提供一些对路的服务。(《在中央党的群团工作会议上的讲话》（2015年7月6日）， 中共中央文献研究室 2017, 197)

How to setup a focus group

  1. Size: 5–15
  2. Subjects read or watch something.
  3. Researcher ask the questions.
  4. Subjects express the feelings and opinions.

Focus group vs. interviews

Pros:

  1. Real-life (conversational) environment
  2. Flexibility
  3. Low cost (< case studies)

Cons:

  1. Non-privacy-protection
  2. Who lead the talk?
  3. Selection interviewees

Understanding Evidence

  • When A is a sufficient condition of B, then once A happens, B must happen.
  • When A is a necessary condition of B, then there must be A for B to happen.

Practice

  • Being 2, 4, 6, or 8 is __ for being an even natural integer.

  • Being 2, 4, 6, or 8 is __ for being an even natural integer <10.

  • Being highly intelligent is __ for being a student of Tsinghua.

  • Showing up to class is __ for getting an A.

  • Being mortal is __ for being human.

  • Being a rational animal is __ for being human.

  1. Hoop test: no alibi, the accused in the state when murder happens? If not innocent, but passing does not mean being guilty. 《顶楼》中女孩儿死时最上面女主在现场，但并不代表就是她造成的女孩儿的死亡（虽然其实是）
  2. Smoking-gun test: a smoking gun seen in a suspect’s hand moments after a shooting is quite conclusive proof of guilt, but a suspect not seen with a smoking gun is not proven innocent.《狂飙》中高起强是白手套，京海市市长兼市委副书记赵立冬才是幕后保护伞
  3. Double-decisive test: hoop + smoking-gun, like a camera record 柯南说是就是
  4. Straw-in-the-wind test: Richard Liu seemed friendly with the girl. 之前女主挽着Richard上电梯，并不代表之后不存在sexual harassment或rape
